How payouts work

When the pool finds a block, your share is recorded as maturing. After 100 confirmations (Bitcoin's coinbase-maturity rule, ~4 hours at 150 s spacing), it moves into spendable balance. The next payout cycle (every 10 minutes) sends spendable balance ≥ your minimum payout to your stealth address as a confidential transaction.
